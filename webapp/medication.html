Common Medications & Pregnancy

General safety categories ‚Äî always verify with your doctor.

Generally Safe

Low Risk

  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
  • Prenatal vitamins
  • Folic acid
  • Iron supplements
  • Certain antacids

Always use as directed and consult your provider.

Use With Caution

Moderate Risk

  • Some antihistamines
  • Certain antibiotics
  • Some blood pressure meds
  • Certain anti-nausea drugs
  • Some topical treatments

Requires provider evaluation for your situation.

Avoid

High Risk

  • NSAIDs (Ibuprofen, Aspirin)
  • Certain acne medications
  • Some seizure medications
  • Certain blood thinners
  • Some herbal supplements

Generally contraindicated during pregnancy.

üìã Tips

Before taking any medication

  • Tell your doctor about all medications you're taking, including OTC and supplements
  • Ask about the FDA pregnancy category for any new medication
  • Never stop prescribed medication without consulting your doctor first
  • Keep a list of all medications to share with your healthcare team
  • Report any side effects immediately to your provider
