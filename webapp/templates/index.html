Maternal Health Tips

Balanced Nutrition

Eat a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Take prenatal vitamins with folic acid.

Stay Active

Aim for 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily. Walking, swimming, and prenatal yoga are excellent choices.

Rest Well

Get 7-9 hours of sleep. Rest on your left side to improve blood flow to your baby.

Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports amniotic fluid levels and reduces swelling.

Regular Checkups

Attend all prenatal appointments. Early detection of issues leads to better outcomes for you and baby.

Mental Wellness

Practice stress-reduction techniques. Talk to loved ones and seek support when needed.
